**Description**

ZeroBite is a full-stack web application developed to reduce food waste by connecting food donors with NGOs and fertilizer organizations. The platform enables individuals, restaurants, hotels, and event organizers to donate surplus food, allowing NGOs to collect and distribute it to people in need before the food expires. Expired or inedible food is redirected to fertilizer organizations for eco-friendly disposal, ensuring sustainable waste management. The application provides a simple, secure, and user-friendly interface for managing food donations while promoting social responsibility and environmental sustainability.

**Objectives**
 -Reduce food wastage.
 -Provide surplus food to people in need.
 -Support sustainable waste management.
 -Connect donors with NGOs efficiently.
 -Promote environmental awareness.

**Features**
**User Module**
 -User registration and login
 -Donate surplus food
 -View donation history
 -Track donation status
 -Manage user profile
**NGO Module**
 -View available food donations
 -Accept food donation requests
 -Update collection status
 -Manage collected food records
**Fertilizer Organization Module**
 -View expired food listings
 -Accept expired food for recycling
 -Update collection status
**Admin Module**
 -Manage users
 -Manage NGOs and fertilizer organizations
 -Monitor all food donations
 -Track donation statistics
 -Manage system records

** Workflow**
 -User registers and logs into the system.
 -User submits surplus food details.
 -Food donation becomes visible to nearby NGOs.
 -NGOs accept and collect the food before its expiry.
 -If food expires before collection, it is automatically moved to the expired food section.
 -Fertilizer organizations can request and collect expired food.Admin monitors all users, donations, and collection activities.
